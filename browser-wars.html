Browser Wars

The Browser Wars refer to periods of intense competition between web browsers, particularly in the late 1990s and early 2000s usage share, driving innovation and standardization.

Key Events

  • 1995-2001: First Browser War between Netscape Navigator and Microsoft Internet Explorer.
  • 2004-2010s: Second Browser War with the rise of Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, and others.
  • Impact: Improved web standards like HTML5 and CSS3.
